TwitPoll

TwitPoll keeps track of the number of Twitter followers for each of the registered UK political parties with elected representation, their leaders and associated youth movements. Some other parties are also included where they form a significant part of the political landscape. For each party, the main Twitter account is used and data is refreshed hourly.

This is an informal measure but gives a perspective on the relative public interest in online communication from each party. If you have any comments or feedback then get in touch on Twitter.
